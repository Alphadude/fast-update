Facts & Questions

Have a question? Read through our FAQ below. If you can't find an answer,
please email our support team. We're here to help.

Simply download the FastBite app, create an account, browse vendors in your area, select your meal, and check out. Your order will be confirmed and delivered to your location.

We’re constantly expanding! FastBite is currently available in major cities and towns. Download the app and turn on location services to see if we operate in your area.

On average, orders are delivered within 30–45 minutes, depending on your location and vendor distance. You can track your delivery in real-time through the app.

We support multiple payment options: debit/credit cards, mobile money, and cash on delivery (where available). Choose your preferred payment method at checkout.

Visit our website or open the app and click “Become a Vendor.” Fill out the registration form, upload your menu and business details, and our team will reach out for onboarding.

If you encounter any issues, you can reach our 24/7 support team via the app's Help section. We’ll resolve your complaint as quickly as possible—your satisfaction is our priority.
